Available Commands

Use these Telegram commands to interact with SUIMEMEBOT and unleash your custom memes. Designed for ease and maximum creativity!

  • /start
    Kicks off your journey with SUIMEMEBOT, welcoming you to the slime crew and showing all commands with customization options.
  • /SUIMEME [scene] [text]
    Creates a meme with the Blue Slime King (or your custom character) in a chosen scene, with optional text (e.g., /SUIMEME underwater "Slime Vibes!").
  • /settings
    Lets you personalize group settings, like picking a custom character, ticker, colors, or themes, via an interactive menu.
  • /sup
    Drops a fun, $SUIMEME-themed greeting from the Blue Slime King to keep the vibes flowing.
  • /how or /help
    Guides you through using SUIMEMEBOT, including tips for customizing and creating memes.
